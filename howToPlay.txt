How to play

When you launch the game, a menu will appear on the screen

=========MENU=========

1. New game
2. Leaderboard
4. Settings
5. Test colors
6. How to play
7. Credits

0. Exit
======================

To start a new game, enter 1. A sub-menu will appear on screen

=======NEW GAME=======

1. 1v1
2. Generate

0. Back
======================

By pressing 2, a game with a generated sequence will start
